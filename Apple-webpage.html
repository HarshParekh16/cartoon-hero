About the iPhone 13 pro max

iPhones are amazing and this year is no exception. Today, we are going to have a look at Apple's 2021 flagship offering, the iPhone 13 pro max!

A side by side battery comparison between last year's iPhone 12 pro max and the iPhone 13 pro max

Specifications

  1. It has a 6.7 inch AMOLED screen that can refresh upto 120 times per second
  2. It is powered by the newest chip from Apple, the A14 bionic, which is 30% more powerful than the competition
  3. It comes with a triple camera setup with lens that allow upto 200% more light to enter, helping achieve the user a better and sharper image
  4. It comes with support for 20w fast charging
  5. It is brighter than any other iPhone with its peak brightness reaching upto 1300 nits
  6. This year's iPhone comes with revolutionary improvements in battery performance, with the iPhone 13 pro max lasting 2 and a half hours longer than last year's iPhone 12 pro max and the iPhone 13 lasting upto 2 hours longer than last year's iPhone 12.
  7. The ProMotion screen as mentioned earlier can refresh upto 120 times per second but its benefits don't end there. It can refresh anywhere between 20 and 120 times per second as per the app's requirement, helping you save your iPhone's battery.
